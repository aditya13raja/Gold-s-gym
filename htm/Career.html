Career

Bodybuilding Coach

A bodybuilding coach works with individuals, athletes, sports teams and competitive bodybuilders to help them achieve their fitness and physique goals. Bodybuilding coaches design weightlifting routines using scientific research to ensure maximum effectiveness.


Dietician

A dietitian is knowledgeable about the dietary needs of anyone who makes fitness a large part of their lives. They can inform and assist clients in developing meal plans based on their caloric output and recommend vitamins and supplements. Their work helps improve performance and manage weight so their clients can live a healthy lifestyle that supports their fitness goals.


Fitness Engineer

A fitness technology engineer'sfitness technology engineer's main responsibility is to study the problems affecting fitness issues and create practical solutions. It is among the highest paying jobs in fitness because the job duty is to solve real-world situations due to a sedentary lifestyle. The engineers also research how current technologies affect their clients' fitness levels and then issue the recommended solutions.


Massage Therapist

A sports massage therapist provides rehabilitation to the sore muscles of fitness seekers by offering deep tissue massage. Their work relieves pain, prevents further injury, increases flexibility and eases knots formed by lactic acid.


Personal Trainer

A certified personal trainer is an accredited expert in fitness training and exercise. They use their knowledge and experience to help their clients achieve their health and fitness goals. They assess their client's current physical conditioning and develop exercise and nutrition plans according to specific goals.
