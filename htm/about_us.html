About Us

Our brand "GOLD'S GYM" was founded in 2015 by three college students named Aditya Raj, Haresh S and Ashutosh Kumar. Gold's gym is equipped with the most latest gym equipments of this century and the cost of the memberships are cheaper than gyms with low quality equipment which gives the customer an advantage to choose Gold's gym for their fitness journey. Each gym trainer in our gyms is a mr India or atleast state level participants,out of which 2 are mr India title winners and 65 are state level fitness winners. Not only our employees participated in fitness competitions, our 767 customers also participated in fitness competitions and 65 of them won the competitions.


We believe that healthy lifestyle lead to better quality of life. The main idea is to have our members enjoy their fitness trainings in classy and private settings. As such, it is not surprising that GOLD'S GYM is passionately led by a team of highly qualified and experienced fitness instructors and health professionals. Our team's passion is to serve our members with sheer dedication, warm friendliness and joyful helpfulness.


It is always better to learn from experts who have accomplished their fitness journey,this is why Gold's gym chooses its trainers more carefully to provide the best assistance for your fitness journey.


We are looking forward to not only train you towards fitness but also for life as if we show high determination towards fitness,then success is all yours.
